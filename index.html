What are Akan Names

In many parts of Ghana, and elsewhere in West Africa, a persons name is determined by the day of the week on which she/he was born. Enter your birthday and gender below to read your day name in Ashanti Twi, a dialect of Akan and the most widely spoken language in Ghana:
